About AGeX

Animation and Game Expo is initiated by the student council of Multimedia and Entertainment Technology Association (META), the official student organization of BS Entertainment and Multimedia Computing (BS EMC). This event is made to showcase the talents and skills, to enhance and empower the creativity and positive attitude of the students, and to encourage aspiring individuals who wishes to pursue their dreams in the field of Multimedia and Gaming Industry.

AGeX Webinars

Through the help of the new technology on giving and spreading knowledge and expertise of different professionals, the AGeX gives free webinars to all interested individuals, students, and professionals. This is to extend the passion of the university and the stakeholders to spread the creativity throughout the nation and to the whole world.

AGeX Exhibit

One of the goals of the Exhibit is to give recognition to the students who made exemplary outputs in their respective field. Also, recognizing the talents and skills of BS EMC Students who has the potential in traditional and digital art, motion graphics, graphic design, character making, game design, game art, and etc.
